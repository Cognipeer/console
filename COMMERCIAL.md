# Commercial Use

Cognipeer Console is available in this repository under AGPL-3.0 for the community edition.

That means commercial use is allowed, but AGPL reciprocity applies when you distribute the software or offer a modified network service.

## When You May Want Commercial Terms

You may want a separate commercial agreement if you need:

- closed-source redistribution,
- OEM or embedded distribution,
- proprietary hosted derivatives,
- contractual support, onboarding, or SLA coverage,
- licensing terms that differ from AGPL reciprocity.

## What To Do

For commercial licensing or support discussions, contact the repository owners through their official Cognipeer channels before shipping a closed-source or OEM derivative.

## Community Edition Reminder

If you are comfortable with AGPL obligations, you can use the repository under the community license without a separate commercial agreement.
